Marketplace Optimization (MpO)
Improve seller performance with this new strategy


What is Marketplace Optimization?

Marketplace Optimization is a revolutionary new concept in the ever changing online marketplace. Emerging software and services are used to optimize a company's sales strategy in order to increase seller ranking on multiple marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, etc.).

A Marketplace Optimization Expert provides insight into the individual ranking algorithms (signals) used by marketplaces to recommend sellers to prospective buyers. By identifying and improving signal performance a company will receive preference, resulting in increased exposure (sales) on a marketplace by winning the "Buy Box".


Increase Sales at:


Ranking Signals

Below is a sample of the signals a marketplace could use to rank sellers:

  • Total Cost to Buyer (including shipping)
  • Overall Order Cancellation Rate of the Seller
  • Shipping Latency
  • History and Experience of the Seller on the Marketplace
  • Overall Customer Feedback
  • Available Inventory for Purchase
  • Order Defect Rate as a % of Gross Sales
  • Late Shipments as a % of Total Shipments
  • Gross Sales
  • Total # of Sales on Marketplace

Marketplaces

Selling on marketplaces can play a key role in a websites success by increasing exposure for more sales opportunities. Zoovy has integrated with the top marketplaces: Amazon, eBay, and Buy.com in an effort to help merchants diversify and sell more online.

Zoovy simplifies and automates the process of listing products directly from a Zoovy web store to multiple marketplaces. Our built in system lets you control which products appear where, how much they sell for, and maintain inventory in real time. Orders and tracking information is also shared between Zoovy and the marketplace automatically. Maintaining everything in one central location saves time and money.

Zoovy can provide live training for any marketplace integration assistance.

Comparison Shopping

Zoovy makes it easy for merchants to increase exposure and visibility to shoppers with our automated product feeds for comparison shopping sites, sometimes referred to as shopping engines. Simply choose the products and shopping engines with a click of the button. The feeds are automated and have the ability to optimize product listings based on unique characteristics of each shopping site. Zoovy runs products through a validation process that ensures the feed quality, allowing more products to get to shopping sites successfully. Built in reporting helps determine how successful the shopping engines are based on performance.

Zoovy integrates and supports the top comparison shopping engines and continually adds emerging comparison shopping sites. Whether it be Amazon Product Ads, Nextag, Pricegrabber, TheFind.com, or Google Products, Zoovy saves you time and money on the backend while making you money on the front.
